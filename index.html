A little about me

Born and raised in Coquitlam, BC. As a kid I was always fasinated by design as well as the art of selling. Relating to the consumers and giving them what they want while also making it visually pleasing. I started my career path doing trade jobs, showing me the brutality of hardwork and dedication to the job. As well improving my teamwork and communication skills. Unfortunately do to a life changing event I switched courses and put all my determination towards my passion for design. I'm young and eager to get into the work force and start making a name for myself. No longer am I in search of just a "job", but a career instead.
